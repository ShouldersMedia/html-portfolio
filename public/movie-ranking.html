Best Christmas Movies According to Alicia!!

My Top 4 Favorite Holiday movies of All Time.

A Christmas Story

It is a fun loving holiday story that reminds me of growing up in the Midwest.

Elf

Will Ferrell is so funny and it is a great laugh while showing the spirit of Christmas.

The Grinch Who Stole Christmas

I just love the innocence of Cindy Lou Who and the friendship she makes with the grinch is priceless.

Home Alone

I love how innovative Kevin is with boobytrapping the house to keep the burglars at bay.
